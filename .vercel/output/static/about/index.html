Our Story

From a Single Cabin to
Alaska's Best.

It started with a single cabin in Fairbanks. Mike and Sarah had watched too many friends visit Alaska and stay in generic hotels, missing out on everything that makes this place extraordinary. So they opened their own cabin doors — and guests started coming back year after year.

Word spread. Other property owners who shared the same philosophy — authentic hospitality, immaculate spaces, real local knowledge — wanted to join. Alaska Stays was born: a curated collection of the state's finest vacation properties, each one personally vetted and owner-operated.

Today we represent over 40 properties across seven regions, from aurora-viewing cabins in the Interior to waterfront lodges on the Kenai. But the mission hasn't changed: help every guest experience the Alaska that locals love.
