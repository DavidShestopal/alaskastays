About this property

Escape to a stunning log cabin retreat in Palmer, where sweeping mountain and valley views create one of the most unforgettable stays in Alaska. This beautifully furnished cabin features three spacious bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms, providing plenty of room for families or groups seeking a peaceful getaway surrounded by nature.

Step outside onto the expansive deck overlooking the Matanuska River Valley, where you can unwind in the private hot tub while taking in panoramic mountain scenery. After a day of adventure, relax in the custom-built cedar sauna or refresh in the luxurious steam shower with an overhead rain feature—a perfect way to recharge in true Alaskan style.
