Common Questions

Quick Answers.

How far in advance should I book?

For peak summer season (June-August), we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. For aurora season (September-March), 1-2 months is usually sufficient.

Do you offer airport pickup?

Many of our hosts offer airport pickup or can arrange transportation. This varies by property — check individual listings or ask when booking.

What's the cancellation policy?

Each property sets its own cancellation policy. Most offer free cancellation up to 5 days before check-in. Check the specific listing for details.

Can I bring my pet?

Some properties are pet-friendly — look for the "Pet Friendly" badge. Pet fees typically range from $50-$100 per stay.

Is it safe to visit Alaska in winter?

Absolutely. Winter is magical — northern lights, dog sledding, hot springs. Our hosts provide detailed safety info and gear recommendations for cold weather visits.
