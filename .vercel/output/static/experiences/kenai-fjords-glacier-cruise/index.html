About This Experience

Join our expert photographers on an unforgettable journey into the Alaskan wilderness to witness and photograph the aurora borealis. We take you to our secret locations away from light pollution, set up professional-grade tripods and camera equipment, and guide you through every setting to capture stunning aurora photographs — whether you're a seasoned photographer or holding a DSLR for the first time.
